💛 At Nordisk Dans, our community runs on the energy and dedication of our volunteers — you are what makes the magic happen. We ask that if you sign up for a shift, you do your best to show up, as the team counts on you. Life happens and we always understand if something comes up, but please be honest with yourself about your availability before signing up. We aim for volunteers to take around two shifts per month, but we'd rather have you when you can than not at all.
